About Us

Who We Are

IFixPhones Repair Shop is a local repair business in Gaborone, Botswana. We help customers fix cracked screens, weak batteries, charging issues, and other common phone problems with honest advice and reliable workmanship.

Our Story

We started with a simple goal: make phone repairs fast, affordable, and easy to understand. Many customers come to us after bad experiences elsewhere, and we focus on clear communication from drop-off to collection.

Our Mission

To return every device in working condition using quality parts, careful repair work, and testing before handover. We believe good service builds long-term trust.

What We Stand For

  • Honest pricing with no hidden fees
  • Quality parts and careful repairs
  • Fast turnaround when possible
  • Friendly, respectful customer service

Need a repair or have a question? We are happy to help.
